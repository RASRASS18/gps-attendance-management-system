# 📖 Platform Overview

GPS Attendance Management System is an enterprise attendance platform developed to digitalize employee attendance, work-location verification, shift scheduling, operational activities, leave administration, and workforce monitoring.

Instead of using manual attendance sheets or traditional fingerprint devices, employees can securely record attendance directly from their smartphones while the system validates their GPS location and operational schedules.

The platform is designed to support organizations with field employees who require attendance verification outside traditional office environments.
